Intro

  • Liuzhe Qiu, postdoctoral researcher at Eastern Institute of Technology, Ningbo, obtained his Doctor of Engineering degree from Hainan University in June 2025, and was a co-taught doctoral student at Tianjin University. He received a master’s degree from Tianjin University in 2020 and a bachelor’s degree from Yantai University in 2017. His research centers on the design of key electrode materials for metal-air batteries, electrolyte modification, and structural optimization. He has authored four research articles published in renowned journals including Angewandte Chemie International Edition and Chemical Engineering Journal, filed six national invention patents, and secured two patent grants. In addition, he has led one postgraduate innovation project in Hainan Province and contributed to one scientific research initiative in Yunnan Province. He is also a reviewer for the journal Progress in Natural Science: Materials International.

Research Field

  1. Development of defect-rich non-noble metal materials;
  2. Investigation of the reaction mechanism of non-noble metal catalysts for ORR;
  3. Electrolyte modification and structural design of metal-air batteries.
