- Yilin Zhang, postdoctoral researcher at Peking University, with a Bachelor's and Ph.D. in Physics from Fudan University. My primary research focuses on the inverse design and property investigation of low-dimensional quantum materials. I employ multi-scale ab initio computational methods to explore the electronic structure, spin characteristics, and transport properties of two-dimensional materials.
