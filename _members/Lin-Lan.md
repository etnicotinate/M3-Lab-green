## Background Information

Lan Lin is an M.S. candidate in Materials Science and Engineering at Lanzhou
University and is currently participating in a joint training program at the
Ningbo Eastern Institute of Technology (EIT). Her research focuses on the
design, development, and performance prediction of solid-state electrolytes
(SSEs). Current work integrates multiscale simulations with hybrid machine
learning models to establish a Composition-Process-Structure-Property
framework for scientific design and optimization of SSE materials. Her
academic background includes experimental synthesis, materials computation,
machine learning modeling, and electrochemical testing.

## Research Field

Solid-state electrolyte materials design, development, and performance
prediction using multiscale simulation and hybrid machine learning.
