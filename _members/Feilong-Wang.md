## Intro

- Feilong Wang, he earned Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Science from Fudan University in June 2024, following the completion of Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) degree at Soochow University in June 2019. Throughout his doctoral studies, he specialized in the field of material photophysics, with a particular emphasis on silicon luminescence and silicon lasers. His research aimed to explore the fundamental mechanisms and practical applications of light-matter interactions in silicon-based materials, contributing to advancements in optoelectronic devices and photonic technologies. His research has resulted in the publication of over 14 SCI-indexed papers in prestigious international journals, including Advanced Science, ACS Photonics, Physical Review B (PRB), Physical Chemistry Chemical Physics (PCCP), The Journal of Physical Chemistry Letters (JPCL), Carbon, and Chemical Engineering Journal (CEJ).

## Research Field

1. Formation Mechanisms and Ion Transport in Amorphous Solid Halide Electrolytes.
2. Chemical Reaction Mechanisms at the Interface of Amorphous Solid Halide Electrolytes and Electrodes.
3. Development and Optimization of Amorphous Solid Halide Electrolytes: A Perspective from First-Principles Calculations and Machine Learning.
