## Intro

- Jiahua Liu earned his Ph.D. degree in Engineering from Peking University in June 2024 after the completion of a B.Eng. degree at Nanchang University in June 2017. Throughout his doctoral studies, he specialized in the field of first-principle computational studies on the mechenical behaviour of layered cathode materials of lithium-ion batteries, with also a deep sduty on elastic theory. After a period of working experience as a postdoctoral research fellow at Peking University Shenzhen Graduate School, he now works at Eastern Institute of Technology (EIT), Ningbo as a postdoctoral researcher. He has published 11 peer-reviewed papaers, including 4 (co-)first-author papers published in *PRX Energy*, *Phys. Chem. Chem. Phys.* (PCCP), *Electrochem. Energ. Rev.* (EER), and *J. Energ. Chem.* (JEC). His current research interests mainly focus on machine learning assisted multiscale computational simulations and design of mechanically stable interfaces in solid-state batteries.

## Research Field

- The mechanical response behavior of inorganic solid electrolyte crystals and amorphous materials.
- Multiscale simulation of the electro-chemo-mechanical couping behaviour at the interface of solid-state batteries.
