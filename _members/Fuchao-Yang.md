## Background Information

Fuchao Yang is an M.S. student in Chemical Engineering at Taiyuan University of
Technology and a joint-training graduate student in the Multiscale Simulation
Materials Laboratory at the Ningbo Eastern Institute of Technology (EIT). He
is advised by Prof. Shoudong Xu (home university) and Prof. Fucheng Ren
(joint-training institute). His research focuses on electrochemical reactions
at battery solid-liquid interfaces, electrolyte reduction pathways, and ion
transport properties using density functional theory and molecular dynamics
simulations. He studies interfacial microscopic mechanisms and optimization
strategies for high-performance battery interfaces, with an interdisciplinary
background in materials science and computational chemistry.

## Research Field

Electrochemical reactions at battery solid-liquid interfaces, electrolyte
reduction pathways, and ion transport properties using density functional
theory and molecular dynamics simulations.
